The Problem

The home mover blind spot is costing you...

A customer changing address is the single most commercially consequential event in retail energy. Yet most suppliers only find out after the keys have changed hands, competing on price comparison at £80–£120 CPA instead of engaging proactively.

For a 275,000-property supplier, that's roughly 1,375 mover events every month, and without advance identification, a significant proportion of those customers will simply not bring their supplier with them.

Today's signals are all lagging

By the time a supplier learns a customer is moving, the decision has already been made.
